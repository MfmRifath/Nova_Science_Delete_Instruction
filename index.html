How to Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go! Follow the steps below to permanently delete your account:

  1. Log In: Sign in to your account using your username and password.
  2. Access Profile: Click on your profile picture or username at the top-right corner of the page, then select Settings from the dropdown menu.
  3. Find Delete Account Option: Scroll down to the bottom of the Account settings page until you see the Delete Account section.
  4. Initiate Deletion: Click on the Delete Account button.
  5. Confirm Deletion: A confirmation dialog will appear. Enter your password and confirm that you want to permanently delete your account.
  6. Final Confirmation: Click on Confirm Deletion to finalize the process.

Note: Deleting your account is irreversible. All your data, including posts, comments, and personal information, will be permanently removed.
